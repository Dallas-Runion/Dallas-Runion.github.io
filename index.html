About me



Theres alot to say about myself becuase everyone in their own is complex. so lets start at my childhood up to my current life. I was born in eastern Kentucky to a family of five. My father was a coal miner and my mother delivered the mail. My early adult life consisted of me enlisting in the US Army at the age of 18. I started out in the Army as an airborne infantryman and then later pivoted to becoming an helicopter mechanic and crew cheif for the uh-60. While I was in the service I've traveled to multiple different countries and picked up alot insight to many cultures and really experinced as much as I could while there as they say "when in Rome". I tend to enjoy the outdoors from my past experinces in the military survival, bushcraft, camping procdures and many more have been aquired. I mostly go outdoors to site see and I really enjoy travel. I'm currently in devcode camp to learn how to become a full stack developer. I'm excited to learn as much as I can. I look forward to the solving problems that arise and really enjoy having to cricitaclly think to solve a problem. I do realize theres alot of knowledge in the coding world but thats a challange I'd like tackle.
